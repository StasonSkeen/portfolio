Shyne

Beginning Process

I started out creating Shyne's single page website by trying to understand the client and their audience, in order to to this I created a project brief. I then created two site maps (Site Map A & Site Map B) to layout the geography of the site.

Wireframing

Wireframe A was my first design and only the social media section of the design was carried through to the next stage of designs. The layout of the site had too much "texture on texture" making the it too visually confusing. Wireframe B had the About Us and Contact page sections move forward to the next design stage. The only thing carried on from Wireframe C were the colours.

Hi-Fi Wireframing

The second round of Wireframes took some of the layouts of the first round and made them less visually confusing by simplifyng the backgrounds. Wireframe D uses a darker theme and the layouts of the About Us, Social Media, and Contact pages used previously. However, a dark theme doesn't fit a Hawaiian/Reggae fusion music duo so this design was not used. The colours from Wireframe E were used while the Contact page changed.

Final

The final design of Wireframe F was a bright red, yellow, green, and black color pallete that matched the aethetic of the music duo's aesthetic.
