Martial Arts Magazine

Typography

Creating a a magazine spread first started with understanding which typefaces would best suit the article. The article was about the Hawaiian Martial Art of Lua so typefaces that emulated a Hawaiian aesthetic would work best. I found that Kabel, as headings, and Optima, as body copy worked best.

Drafts

I went through many revisions of the magazine before landing on a great design. Some of the design problems came from a bad composition of typography and imagery. For example, the table of contents page has very cramped typography and dull imagery backing it up.
