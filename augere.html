Augere App

Branding

The branding guide of the company is included here. The Augere brand is where sci-fi futuristic innovation meets the Renaissance.

Site Map

Creating the site map in order to see the geography and direction of the app. This allows me to plan where I want the user to navigte throughout the application as well as where content will be laid out throughout the app.

Draft 1

Augere is a science fiction comapny that provides robotic prothetics to amputees. This mock phone application is meant to be a way for the consumers of Augere products to use in unison with their prosthetics. This first draft of the app were both off brand for the company as well as not having well enough contrast between colours.

Draft 2

The second draft was more on brand for the company however the buttons still looked clunky and were not displaying Augere's mix of both futurism with Renaissance aesthetic.
